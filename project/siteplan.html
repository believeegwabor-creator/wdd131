Kingsmen Site Plan

Kingsmen Laundry Services

I chose this name because it represents professionalism, quality service, and reliability. The name "Kingsmen" gives the business a premium and trustworthy identity while clearly representing a modern laundry service.

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about laundry and dry-cleaning services. Visitors will be able to learn about pricing, pickup and delivery options, business hours, and contact information.

Scenarios

  • How much does laundry pickup and delivery cost?
  • Where can I contact the laundry service quickly?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#1E88E5) - Used for headings, navigation, and buttons.
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5) - Used for the page background and sections.

Typography

  • Montserrat - Used for headings.
  • Roboto - Used for body text and paragraphs.
